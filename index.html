What's This Website About?

My name's Lori, and this is the website I created for English 3844. This class taught me the skills necessary to make my own website that features products I've written, recorded, and filmed! This platform will not only share what I've worked on all semester, it will also include links and information about influential entertainment/music/art venues in the Blacksburg area. My goal is to inform and educate anyone interested in what I'm passionate about: creativity and the arts. Click around and explore-- you just might enjoy yourself!
